How to Set Up
1
App Intents Framework
Miss M registers AppIntent structs — Xcode 15, macOS 14+. Siri discovers them automatically.
2
No config needed
Once the app is built, "Hey Siri, ask Miss M…" works immediately. No Shortcuts app setup required.
3
Works from anywhere
iPhone, Mac, AirPods, HomePod, Apple Watch, CarPlay — any Apple device she owns.
4
Custom phrases
She can create custom shortcut phrases in the Shortcuts app: "Ask Miss M my day" → morning briefing
