How Nike Uses the STP Model to Dominate Global Markets
Nike's remarkable market dominance can be traced to its sophisticated application of the Segmentation, Targeting and Positioning (STP) framework. Rather than adopting a mass-market approach, Nike has strategically divided its global customer base into distinct segments defined not merely by demographics, but by psychographic and behavioural characteristics — particularly lifestyle, athletic aspiration and brand affinity.

Through its "Just Do It" positioning, Nike speaks not to a specific sport, but to the inner athlete in every consumer — a masterstroke of emotional brand positioning that has proven universally resonant across age groups, geographies and income levels...
