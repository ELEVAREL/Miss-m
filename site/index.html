Everything she does

Native Apple integrations — Calendar, Reminders, HealthKit, PDFKit, Vision, Notes — all wired into a conversational interface.

Streaming chat

Full Claude Sonnet conversations with tool-use, voice input, and seven distinct chat states that actually feel alive.

Calendar & reminders

Reads and writes EventKit. Ask "what's today?" or "remind me to email Dr. Reyes tomorrow at 9."

School toolkit

Kanban for assignments, essay writer with citations, flashcards, Pomodoro, and Marketing frameworks (SWOT, STP, PESTLE).

Smart Life Planner

A 7-day plan stitched from your calendar, cycle, sleep, meals, and budget. Ask her to adjust it in plain English.

Smart Fitness

Workout suggestions that follow your energy and cycle phase. Yoga when you need it, HIIT when you can take it.

Home & life

Meal planner, grocery list, budget tracker, email drafter with tone selector — everything a home manager actually needs.

Wellness & cycle

Reads HealthKit (including Flo-synced data). Sleep, steps, heart rate, HRV, cycle phase — with mood correlation.

📄

PDF & OCR

Drop a lecture PDF or a screenshot — PDFKit and Vision OCR extract the text, Claude summarises and builds flashcards.
